— 01 / Studio

Welcome to Pavilion Pilates.

Our story

Founded in 2025, we are nestled in the heart of Fort Lee — a sanctuary where we curate transformative mind-body experiences.

Our studio integrates modern and classical Pilates teaching systems to help clients achieve their training goals.

We create a Positive Zone where you can transform your energy, your flow, to shape your reality.

What makes us different

  • We offer Private & Duet & Trio (1V3 Group) Pilates Sessions to build strength, flexibility, and mindful movement.
  • We offer Private & Duet Yoga Sessions that can be fully tailored to your fitness goals and body needs.
  • Special pre- and postnatal combined yoga and Pilates Session to guide new mothers in maintaining fitness.
  • Our certified instructors guide you with personalized attention in a warm, inclusive environment.
  • Sustainable studio with premium props.
  • Access unique workshops you won't find in regular classes.
  • Bilingual instruction.

— 06 / Policy

Before you arrive.

Quick answers to what new clients usually ask. Anything else, the front desk is at +1 (551) 333-5081.

What should I bring to my first class?+
Just yourself in comfortable, fitted clothing. We provide mats, grip socks (your first pair is on us), and all Pilates apparatus. Bring a water bottle — we have a refill station in the lobby.
I've never done Pilates. Is TRIO the right starting point?+
For most beginners — yes. TRIO 1:3 is small enough for the teacher to correct form individually but social enough to be welcoming. If you have a significant injury or specific rehabilitation goal, we'll route you to a Private 1:1 first.
Do you offer prenatal and postnatal sessions?+
Yes. Scarlett, Julia, and Judy are all certified in pre/postnatal movement. Mama Movement (Mondays + Thursdays) is a small dedicated class; private sessions are also available throughout pregnancy and the fourth trimester.
What is the cancellation policy?+
Cancel free up to 6 hours before class. Cancellations inside 6 hours are charged in full so we can hold space for waitlisted clients. Memberships pause for up to 4 weeks per year.
Where do I park?+
Free 90-minute street parking on Central Rd and side streets. A municipal lot is one block south on Lemoine Ave. Reformer-class clients are welcome to load gear from the side entrance.
Do you teach in languages other than English?+
Yes — instruction is available in English, Mandarin (Scarlett, Sydney, Cindy), and Korean (Judy). Look for the language badge on each schedule slot.
